Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 10 June 2025

1. Introduction

PocketStat is a mobile app that allows users to monitor their own self-hosted Glances instances. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use the app.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address through Firebase Authentication.
  • Usage Information: We may collect anonymous data to understand how the app is used, including basic diagnostic data.
  • Bug Report Data: If you submit a bug report with a screenshot or photo, that image is stored temporarily in Firebase Storage.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to serve ads to free users. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers, usage data, and device info per Google's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Authenticate and manage user accounts
  • Improve app performance and support
  • Serve relevant advertisements (for free users)
  • Identify and fix bugs based on user-submitted feedback

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • User accounts that remain inactive for more than 90 days may be permanently deleted, including all associated data (e.g. settings, bug reports).
  • You may also request account and data deletion at any time by contacting us at info@spherabytes.com.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following services, which may collect and process data according to their own policies:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage) — Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

PocketStat is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise your rights, contact us at info@spherabytes.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page, and your continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
