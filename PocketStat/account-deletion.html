Account Deletion

Effective Date: 10 June 2025

1. How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your PocketStat account and all associated data at any time directly from within the app.

To do so, follow these steps:

  • Open the PocketStat app on your device.
  • Navigate to Settings > Account.
  • Select Delete Account and confirm when prompted.

Once deleted, all user-specific data including authentication credentials, connected server records, and stored metrics will be permanently removed from our system.

2. Need Help?

If you experience any issues with account deletion or have further questions, please contact our support team at info@spherabytes.com.
